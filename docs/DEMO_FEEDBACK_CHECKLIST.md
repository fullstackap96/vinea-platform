# Vinea Platform — Demo Feedback Checklist

## After every demo, capture:

### 1. First Reaction
- What was their immediate reaction?
- Did they say something like:
  - “This is exactly what we need”
  - or “This is interesting…”

### 2. Confusion Points
- Where did they get confused?
- What did they ask you to repeat?
- What didn’t “click” immediately?

### 3. Most Valuable Feature (to them)
- What did they react most strongly to?
  - Follow-Up Queue?
  - Organization?
  - Intake forms?
  - AI?

### 4. What they expected but didn’t see
- Did they ask:
  - “Can it do X?”
  - “What about Y?”

These are gold.

### 5. Objections / Concerns
- Pricing?
- Data/privacy?
- Ease of use?
- Setup effort?

### 6. Language they used
- Write down exact phrases they use:
  - “We usually track this in email”
  - “Things fall through the cracks”
  - “We lose track of follow-ups”

This becomes your future marketing copy.

### 7. Would they use it?
- Strong yes / maybe / no
- Why?

---

## After 3–5 demos, review patterns:

- What comes up repeatedly?
- What feature is always mentioned?
- What confusion happens every time?

👉 That tells you what to fix or build next
